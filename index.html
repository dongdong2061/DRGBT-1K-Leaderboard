Metrics

Metric Definitions and Resources

PR

Precision Rate, typically measured as the hit rate under a 20-pixel center error threshold.

NPR

Normalized Precision Rate, computed from the normalized center error curve.

SR

Success Rate, derived from the IoU success curve and useful for judging overall overlap quality.
